Ethio-American Business and Industry Show 2024.

The Ethiopian Business and Industry Show in Washington, DC, showcased a variety of key highlights, reflecting the dynamic nature of Ethiopian business and the opportunities available for investment and collaboration. The show featured numerous exhibitors from various sectors, including textiles, agro-processing, ICT, and energy. The show also served as a platform for cultural exchange, showcasing Ethiopian heritage and fostering a deeper understanding of the country's rich cultural landscape.

Ethio American Chamber.

The Ethio-American Chamber of Commerce (EACC) is a, 501(c)(6), non-profit organization established by business owners for the purpose of advancing the interest of members and nurturing a vibrant business community.

The 1st Ethio-America Food and Beverage Show 2023

The Ethio-America Food and Beverage Show is an annual event serves as a vital platform for industry professionals, businesses, and enthusiasts from Ethiopia and the United States to connect, exchange knowledge, and explore opportunities within the food and beverage sector. The event attracts key stakeholders, including manufacturers, distributors, investors, government officials, restaurants & bars as well as consumers, making it an ideal platform to showcase your brand and expand your network.

Objectives of the event.

8th Oct, 2023 - Silver Spring, MD

Cultural exchange and Business opportunities.

Fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation of Ethiopian and American culinary traditions, promoting cultural exchange and dialogue between the two cultures.

Facilitate networking and business connections among food and beverage professionals, including producers, distributors, suppliers, and industry experts, to explore partnerships and expand market reach.

8th Oct, 2023 - Silver Spring, MD

Advocacy and Educational Awareness

Presenting the challenges of the Ethio-American restaurants & bar businesses and discuss the solution with key industry players and officials.

Educate attendees about the history, traditions and unique aspects of Ethiopian and American food and beverages through cooking demonstrations, workshops and informative sessions.

Consumer Engagement.

Raise awareness about Ethiopian cuisine, its health benefits, and its potential as an emerging culinary trend in the American market, creating opportunities for Ethiopian food entrepreneurs and businesses.
